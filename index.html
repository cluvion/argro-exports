🎯 Primary Objective

Generate qualified B2B inquiries and export leads from European buyers — importers, distributors, wholesalers, and food vendors across the UK, Germany, France, Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Belgium, and Scandinavia.

07 · Service 04

Video Production Plan

Strategic video content to build trust with European buyers. Available in 4 production formats — from cost-effective AI/faceless videos to premium UGC-style content.

Faceless Video
Faceless / Stock + Voiceover
Professional stock footage + branded graphics + AI or human voiceover. No on-camera person needed. Best for product explainers and company overview videos.
Duration: 60–90 seconds · Script included
AI Video
AI-Generated Video
100% AI-produced using tools like HeyGen, Synthesia or Runway. AI avatars, AI voice, AI visuals. Ultra cost-effective for educational and informational content.
Duration: 45–75 seconds · Multiple languages possible
UGC Style
UGC (User Generated Content)
Real person on camera, natural authentic style. Shot with phone or basic setup. High trust for B2B buyers — shows real people behind the brand. Filmed by your team or hired creator.
Duration: 45–60 seconds · Full editing included
AI UGC
AI UGC (Synthetic Creator)
AI-generated human avatar that speaks naturally on camera. Looks and feels like real UGC but produced entirely with AI. Works perfectly for EN/DE/FR language variations and EU market ad testing.
Duration: 30–60 seconds · Multi-language variants
